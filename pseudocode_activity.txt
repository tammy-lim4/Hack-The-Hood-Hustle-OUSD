    a. If creating a new alarm:
        a. Set the alarm time (AM/PM)
            a. Make sure you have correct AM/PM

    b. Show snooze button
        a. Give more 5 more minutes
        b. Give more 10 more minutes

        Once extra time is up:
            a. Give "x" amount of minutes
            b. Stop the alarm
    c. Stop the Alarm and get up
